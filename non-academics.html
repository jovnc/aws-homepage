Non-Academics

Apart from academic activities, I also participate in many non-academic activities.

Football

I have always been an avid football fan since young, as I would watch football matches live every weekend night with my father. When I first started watching football, Manchester United were in their prime, winning back-to-back Premier league titles and I have since been a fan of Manchester United, despite losing their form in the recent years.

This passion and love for football led me to join football for my 2 years in JC. However, due to COVID-19, we were unable to have competitions, and had to settle for friendly matches within ourtselves.

Volleyball

I have been playing volleyball since I was P3 in Ai Tong Secondary school, where I was the setter and vice captain of the team during P5 and P6. Moving on, I continued with volleyball for 4 years in secondary school in HCI, where I played as the libero for all 4 years.

Due to my small physique, I never really had an advantage is spiking and blocking, therefore I relied heavily on my defending abilities and ability to get low and save the balls. Therefore, I have been playing as a libero for most of my time in secondary school.
