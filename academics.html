Academics

I recently graduated from HCJC and took A levels for BCME/GP in 2021.

Math

Math is arguably my strongest subject, as I have been consistently scoring well for Math since Primary School. Math requires analytical skills and logical thinking, which comes from frequent exposure and practice. Therefore, I make it a habit to practice Math on a daily basis to keep my mind active and fresh.

Since the end of A levels, I have picked up Math tutoring as a side hustle, either via answering queries from students online via Superstar Mentor or 1-on-1 tuition.

Final A Level Grade: A

Biology

Biology is the most content-heavy subject in JC, with over 20 chapters and hundreds of pages worth of notes to internalise. At the beginning, Biology was a struggle for me, as I was dealing with the subject wrongly. I only memorised the content, and did not fully understand what I was memorising, therefore when it came to exams, I was unable to answer alot of application-based questions, which cost me greatly.

However, after persistence and constant practice and revision, I finally gained confidence and understood the content for A levels. It became one of my more confident subjects in A levels.

Final A Level Grade: A

Chemistry

Chemistry was also one of my strong subjects as I have been consistenly scoring well in Chemistry since Secondary school. My fundamentals for Chemistry was strong since Secondary School, therefore leading to a smooth transition towards JC as most content learnt in JC were an addition to the knowledge in Secondary School. Chemistry is a subject that cannot be memorised and needs to be understood fully before you can apply your concepts in exams to score well.

Since A levels ended, I still try to retain my knowledge in Chemistry by helping students with their Chemistry homework and questions via Superstar Mentor.

Final A Level Grade: A

Economics

Economics was my only humanities subject in A levels. I have been struggling with humanities since Secondary school, where I took History in lower secondary and Geography in lower and upper secondary. Economics was vastly different from History and Geography in secondary school, because at the JC level, we were not expected to simply regurgitate the answer, but also go deeper into the analytical level and answer based on the question application.

While Economics has been challenging for me, I found structure in what I was learning through Zenith Education tuition. They made me understand Economics to a greater degree and allowed me to excel in my A levels.

Final A Level Grade: A

General Paper

General Paper is the hardest subject for me as I have been consistently scoring Bs and Cs for internal school examinations, and language has always been a challenge for me since young as I have troubles expressing my thoughts out loud and in paper. I focused the most in General Paper for my A levels, however I was unable to turn my fortune and ended up with a meagre score of B at the end, which dashed my hopes of getting that 90RP. It is definitely one of my biggest regrets for A levels in 2021.

Final A Level Grade: B
